## What this is

**9.8** covers **provisional** (intermediate) results and published statistics: when they are deemed **published**, when the clock starts for **challenges**, and when outcomes become **final** if no timely protest. The bay **RO** usually does not run the leaderboard but should know the **signed scoresheet** is the key input to that system.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Avoid promising** division placement “from the tablet” — point to the official **9.8** / **MD** channel.
2. **Supply clean line data** so provisional results are not riddled with entry errors.
3. **If an error appears after publication** — route to **scoring / RM** under **9.7** and **9.8**, not silent app edits.
4. **Do not pressure** scoring to move placements without process.
5. **Explain** where official provisional results live (site, app).
