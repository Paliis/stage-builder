## What this is

**5.3** **IPSC Handgun** sets **clothing** requirements for competitors: footwear, gloves, branding / logos, bib visibility, etc. Goals are **safety** (grip on uneven ground), **identification**, and **sponsor neutrality**. Details are **only** from the **Jan 2026** PDF and **MD** (extra range bans).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Check** at registration / pre-start what matters on the line (bib, obviously unsafe shoes, banned markings — per rules).
2. **Do not DQ** for “style” outside the rulebook — borderline cases to **RM**.
3. **Remind** of **MD** add-ons (e.g. covered shoulders in certain bays) if briefed.
4. **During the COF**, clothing rarely stops a run — unless it creates **hazard** (laces in trigger, etc.) — then **STOP** and **RM** assessment.
5. **Apply** the same standards to everyone.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Anchor: **5.3**; cross-check **5.4** (eye protection as safety gear).

## Local (FPSU)

FPSU may require specific jersey rules or face-cover limits — match regs. **`fpsu_delta_verified`**: **false** until **RO_HELPER_V0 §4.1** review.

## Common mistakes

- **Double standards** for “stars” vs newcomers.
- **Appearance comments** instead of citing a rule.
- **Ignoring** unsafe footwear on wet ground.
