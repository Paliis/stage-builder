## What this is

Two related but **different** situations in **Handgun Section 8.7**:

- **8.7.1** ‚Äî behaviour **before the start signal** at the make-ready line (including aiming / ‚Äúsight picture‚Äù per PDF wording).
- **8.7.2** ‚Äî limits during the **official COF inspection** / **walkthrough** (what may be done with the handgun and body on the stage before competition).

Outcomes (e.g. **procedural**, repeat offences, other) are **only** from the **Jan 2026** PDF. Broader walkthrough issues may also appear under **8.7.3** ‚Äî read the full sub-section.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Identify the phase:** make-ready line (**8.7.1**) vs group inspection / walkthrough (**8.7.2**).
2. **Record observations:** muzzle, finger, sighting, testing positions, etc. ‚Äî only what is visible from the **RO‚Äôs allowed position**.
3. **Check the PDF:** which sub-rule applies; any exception (e.g. specific commands ‚Äî per rule text).
4. **Apply the sanction** per **8.7.1** or **8.7.2** (and **8.7.3** if relevant).
5. **Brief the competitor** briefly; **CRO/RM** if interpretation is disputed.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **8.7** as a whole: the PDF defines the line between preparation and violation.
- Do not confuse with **Chapter 10** safety **DQ** issues ‚Äî if unsafe, follow safety escalation and **RM** involvement.

## Common mistakes

- **Mixing 8.7.1 and 8.7.2:** different phases ‚Äî different rule text.
- **Penalty without fact:** ‚Äúlooked like aiming‚Äù without a clear observation ‚Äî weak under protest.
- **Ignoring 8.7.3:** some walkthrough scenarios may sit there.
