## What this is

**Unauthorized assistance** is when someone helps the competitor in a way that violates **Section 8.6** of the rules. Sub-rule **8.6.2** in **IPSC Handgun Jan 2026** covers a specific case / consequence — **always** read the **full** **8.6** text in the PDF (what **RO**, **CRO**, range staff, and bystanders may or may not do).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record:** who assisted; what happened (words, gesture, physical contact, object); when relative to the **COF** as the rule requires.
2. **Do not solo-call** borderline cases that may touch **DQ** or complex interpretation — escalate to **RM** / **CRO** per match procedure.
3. **Apply the sanction** strictly per **8.6.2** (and related **8.6** sub-rules) after PDF verification.
4. **Provide a factual description** for the record / scoresheet (no hearsay; observations only).
5. **Brief the competitor** with the minimum needed; challenges per **9.6**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Anchor: **8.6.2** in the context of all of **8.6** (assistance, coaching, gear, etc. — per PDF).
- Do not replace **8.6.2** with generic **10.1** when the rulebook routes the outcome to **8.6**.

## Local (FPSU)

FPSU range conduct rules may add constraints; verify official FPSU text. **`fpsu_delta_verified`**: **false** until **RO_HELPER_V0 §4.1** review.

## Common mistakes

- **“Friendly” tips:** even a small hint may fall under **8.6** — do not normalize “everyone does it”.
- **Late RM notification:** if DQ risk or systemic unfairness — escalate quickly.
- **Vague scoresheet:** “assistance” with no act and timing — not auditable.
