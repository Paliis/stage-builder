## What this is

**Significant advantage** is a **material unfair gain** during the **COF**—often by shooting from a position that improves access to targets beyond **WSB** limits. **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** routes these cases through **10.2.1.1** and **10.2.2** in **Chapter 10**. Compact stages and junior competitors do not change the need to cite **Mini Rifle** text — do not substitute **Rifle** or **Handgun** PDFs.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** body / gun position vs **WSB** zones.
2. **Escalate** unclear calls to **CRO** / **RM** before finalising.
3. **Apply** **10.2.1.1** / **10.2.2** per verified PDF — not generic **10.1** when advantage clauses apply.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **Document** shots and WSB references for coach / parent review when relevant.
