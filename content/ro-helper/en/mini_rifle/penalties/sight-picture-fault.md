## What this is

Two related but **different** situations in **Mini Rifle Section 8.7**:

- **8.7.1** вЂ” behaviour **before the start signal** at the make-ready line (including aiming / вЂњsight pictureвЂќ per PDF wording вЂ” optics, iron sights, small long-gun handling).
- **8.7.2** вЂ” limits during the **official COF inspection** / **walkthrough** (what may be done with the **mini rifle** and body on the stage before competition).

Outcomes are **only** from the **Jan 2026 Mini Rifle** PDF. Broader walkthrough issues may also appear under **8.7.3** вЂ” read the full sub-section. Use **Mini Rifle** **Chapter 8**, not **Handgun** or full **Rifle** alone.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Identify the phase:** make-ready line (**8.7.1**) vs group inspection / walkthrough (**8.7.2**).
2. **Record observations:** muzzle, finger, sight alignment, testing positions вЂ” only what is visible from the **ROвЂ™s allowed position**.
3. **Check the PDF:** which sub-rule applies; any exception per rule text.
4. **Apply the sanction** per **8.7.1** or **8.7.2** (and **8.7.3** if relevant).
5. **Brief the competitor** briefly; **CRO/RM** if interpretation is disputed.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **8.7** as a whole in the **Mini Rifle** PDF.
- Do not confuse with **Chapter 10** safety **DQ** issues вЂ” if unsafe, follow safety escalation and **RM** involvement.
