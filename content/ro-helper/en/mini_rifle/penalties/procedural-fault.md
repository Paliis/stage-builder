## What this is

A **procedural penalty** applies for **COF** / **WSB** procedure breaches when no more specific **Chapter 10** clause exclusively governs the situation. **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** frames this under **10.1** (read **10.1.1–10.1.2**). **Mini Rifle** is not a “small Rifle” photocopy — point values, counting, and caps come **only** from the **Mini Rifle** PDF.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record facts** with WSB references and where in the **COF** the breach occurred.
2. **Prefer specific 10.2.x** rules when they clearly fit (reload, position, movement, etc.).
3. **Apply** the scoresheet count per **10.1** + any specific clause.
4. **Brief briefly** on the line; challenges via **9.6** / **RM**.
5. **Write auditable notes** for junior squads and parents reviewing scores.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.1** — general procedural frame for **Mini Rifle**; verify **10.1.2** point value.
- **Procedural cap** — **10.2.3** when applicable.

## Common mistakes

- **Opening Rifle** PDF because the gun “looks like a rifle”.
- **Assuming** identical **−10** values without reading **Mini Rifle 10.1.2**.
- **Double penalties** for one act.
