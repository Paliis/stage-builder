## What this is

**Creeping** is prohibited **movement** or **lean** of body or **mini rifle** **before** the start signal that improves readiness for the first shot. **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** addresses this under **10.2.6** (confirm wording, exceptions, and relation to **Make ready** / **Are you ready** in your PDF). Smaller long guns and junior-friendly ready positions still require **Mini Rifle** **Chapter 10**, not **Handgun** or full **Rifle** assumptions.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Watch** the ready position and command sequence per match SOP and **WSB** extras.
2. **Record** movement before **Start**; note stock, muzzle, or foot shifts typical of mini-rifle bays.
3. **Apply** **10.2.6** after PDF check; escalate to **CRO** / **RM** if borderline.
4. **Brief briefly** what was seen.
5. **Document** timing relative to commands and stage ID.

## Common mistakes

- **Using Handgun** creeping cues on a **mini rifle** line.
- **Treating normal** height or stance micro-adjustments as creeping without PDF support.
- **Confusing** creeping with **trigger prep** under other **10.5.x** rules.
