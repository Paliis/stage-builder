## What this is

A **foot fault** is **out-of-area ground contact** or a **fault-line** breach during the **COF** contrary to the **WSB** or general line rules. **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** anchors this under **10.2.1** (see **10.2.1.1** in your PDF). Compact rifles and junior-friendly stages can make **tight** boxes — still use **Mini Rifle** wording, not **Rifle** or **Handgun** PDFs.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** which line, which contact, when in the **COF**, and relation to shots if **10.2.1.1** may apply.
2. **Verify the WSB** activates that line for the position or movement segment.
3. **Apply** **10.2.1** / **10.2.1.1** on the scoresheet per PDF — avoid mis-bucketing as **10.1**.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **On junior squads**, keep notes especially clear for coaches reviewing scores.

## Common mistakes

- **Judging Mini Rifle** with **Rifle** page numbers from memory.
- **Defaulting to 10.1** when **10.2.1** clearly applies.
- **Unclear scoresheet** — no line ID or shot index.
