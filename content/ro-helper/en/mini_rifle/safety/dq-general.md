## What this is

**Section 10.3** of **IPSC Mini Rifle** sets out **general** **DQ** rules for this division. **Specific** breaches remain in **10.4**, **10.5**, etc. **10.3** tells **how** to process the DQ. For minors, federation policies may add steps — they **add to**, not replace, **10.3**. **C130** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Follow 10.3** plus the **substantive** rule that caused the DQ.
2. **Notify** coach / guardian per **MD** if required after a **DQ** to a junior.
3. **RM** involvement when **10.3** or child-protection policy requires it.
4. **Documentation** suitable for protests and federation records.
