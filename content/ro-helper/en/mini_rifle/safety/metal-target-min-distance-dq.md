## What this is

**10.4.7** in **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** covers **disqualification** when a competitor engages **steel** from closer than the distance set in that PDF (and any combined conditions — **read the full sub-clause**). **C310** = **Handgun** — **Mini Rifle** is **not** **full Rifle** or **handgun**; use the **Mini Rifle** PDF for thresholds and definitions.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Check WSB and layout:** distances to poppers / plates vs **10.4.7** and **MD** briefing.
2. **Stop the COF** if mid-string it becomes clear the competitor **violated** minimum distance — then **RM** for **DQ** under **10.4.7** (per PDF).
3. **Do not “measure by eye”** for the official call — involve **RM** / tools if the match defines a method.
4. **Warn MD** before the match if a stage is **impossible** without breaking **10.4.7**.
5. **Document** for the DQ record.

## Common mistakes

- **Full Rifle** distances copied to **Mini Rifle** without checking the **Mini Rifle** PDF.
- **Publishing** a stage that forces a shot inside the **Mini Rifle** limit.
- **Warning instead of DQ** when the PDF requires **10.4.7**.
