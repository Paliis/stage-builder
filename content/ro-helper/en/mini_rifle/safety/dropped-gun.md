## What this is

A **dropped firearm** under **IPSC Mini Rifle** is the same **safety** category: the rifle **unintentionally released** and contacts the ground per **10.5.3** **Mini Rifle** **Jan 2026**. Smaller rifles and junior competitors do **not** change the **rulebook** test — only the **PDF** does. **C120** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **STOP** the COF; **no** hero catches; keep the squad behind cover lines.
2. **Coach / parent** proximity — helpful for welfare, not for bypassing **RM** pickup rules.
3. **Document** for minors: **RM** and **MD** child-safety steps per federation policy.
4. **DQ** under **10.5.3** when the facts match the rule; **no** informal waivers.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.5.3 Mini Rifle**; verify exceptions in **that** book (not **Rifle** PDF by habit).
