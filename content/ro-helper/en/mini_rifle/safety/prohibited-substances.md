## What this is

**10.7** **IPSC Mini Rifle** applies the **prohibited substances** rule to this division. **Junior** competitors are covered by the **same** **IPSC** standard — the **Mini Rifle** PDF defines **DQ** and process. **C135** = handgun. Federation policies for minors may add steps; they must align with **MD** and law.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Immediate RM** if there is a **safety** concern with an impaired junior on the line.
2. **Coach / guardian** contact per **MD** only — not as punishment theatre on the bay.
3. **Document** facts briefly and hand off — **no** medical opinions.
