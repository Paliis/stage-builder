## What this is

**10.5.1** in **IPSC Mini Rifle** is the **general** unsafe-handling clause when facts do not clearly map to a **specific** **10.5** or **10.4** rule. Junior competitors follow the **same** safety standard — the **Mini Rifle** PDF decides **DQ** bases. **C125** = handgun. Do not default to **10.5.1** to avoid naming a specific breach.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Stop** the COF if there is an immediate hazard.
2. **Check** **10.4**, **10.5.2**, **10.5.3**, **10.5.9–11** first.
3. **RM** involvement for minors per **MD** / federation policy.
4. **Written facts** — coaching noise is not a substitute for rule text.
