## What this is

**Chapter 2** **IPSC Mini Rifle** defines **range** zones and **Safety Area** rules for this division. **Junior** squads need the same **clear** boundaries — **Ch.2** **Mini Rifle** PDF is authoritative. **C145** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Visible signage / briefing:** parents and juniors must know **hot** lines and **ammo** rules.
2. **Stop** zone breaches **before** they become **Ch.10** incidents.
3. **RM** if the range layout is ambiguous for young shooters.
