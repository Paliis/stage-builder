## What this is

**10.6** **IPSC Mini Rifle** applies **unsportsmanlike conduct** rules to juniors and this division. **Parents / coaches** can also trigger **MD** involvement if they interfere with officials — follow **10.6** and **MD** child-protection policy. **C140** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Facts to RM:** avoid arguing with minors or adults on the clock — document and escalate.
2. **Separate** coaching passion from **10.6** breaches — still needs **PDF** fit.
3. **Federation** reporting if required after a **DQ** or serious incident.
