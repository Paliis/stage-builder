## What this is

An **accidental discharge (AD)** under **IPSC Mini Rifle** is defined in **Section 10.4** of the **Mini Rifle** **Jan 2026** PDF. Same conceptual role as other divisions: shots **outside** the controlled **COF** fire protocol or other **listed** cases → usually **DQ**, subject to **10.4** exceptions in that book. See **C110** (handgun) for parallel topic only — apply **Mini Rifle** rules on course.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **STOP / cease fire**; safe distance; watch **muzzle** (junior divisions — same safety standard).
2. **Freeze** handling per MD until **RM** if required.
3. **Record** facts: timing, ready condition, coach / parent proximity if relevant to witnesses, RO commands.
4. **DQ** processing per **10.4** and **RM** procedure.
5. **Escalate** to **RM** for minors’ incidents per MD / federation policy.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)


### 10.4.2 — shot into the ground (distance)

- **10.4.2**: a shot **into the ground** within **3 meters** (3 m) — **DQ** per the rule.

- Read **10.4** in **Mini Rifle** completely; do not copy-paste from **Rifle** or **PCC** PDFs.
