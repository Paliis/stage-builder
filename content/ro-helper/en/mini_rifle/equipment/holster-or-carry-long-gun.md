## What this is

**Mini Rifle** **Ch.5** sets **sling** and **carry** rules sized for **junior** athletes — same **safety** bar as adults. **Parents** may not “hold” the rifle for the competitor during **COF** unless **MD** allows a **coach** role. **C235** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Ensure** **slings** are adjusted so **muzzle** stays **controlled** for smaller bodies.
2. **Stop** **unsafe** carry — **no** “it’s just a kid” exceptions.
3. **Escalate** **gear** that looks like **full Rifle** parts in a **Mini** division wrongly.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.5 Mini Rifle**; compare **full Rifle** **Ch.5** only where **MD** aligns rules.
