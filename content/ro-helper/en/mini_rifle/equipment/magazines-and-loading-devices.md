## What this is

**Chapter 5** and **division appendices** limit **Mini Rifle magazines** (length, capacity, count on the person) and **loading devices** (pouches, loaders, etc.). The **RO** may spot obvious oversize mags or extra carriers vs **division**; exact measurement uses **MD** templates / **RM**. **C250** = **Handgun** — use the **Mini Rifle** PDF for **Chapter 5** numbers (do not mix with **full Rifle** limits).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Compare** magazines to what the division allows (confirm in **Mini Rifle** PDF + appendix).
2. **Watch** magazine / pouch counts during **equipment check** or pre-start if **MD** requires it.
3. **Do not allow** obviously non-compliant extensions if the division bans them.
4. **During the COF**, a dropped magazine may trigger **other** rules (**10.5.x**) — do not confuse with capacity / length rules.
5. **Escalate** disputed measurements to **RM**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Chapter 5 Mini Rifle** + Appendix; cross-check **8.1** (loaded magazine in gun on the line) as in Mini Rifle rulebook.

## Common mistakes

- **Using** handgun or **full Rifle** **Chapter 5** limits **without** opening **Mini Rifle** PDF.
- **Confusing** allowed belt magazine count with minimum **WSB** round count.
- **RO** declaring hardware illegal without **RM** process.
