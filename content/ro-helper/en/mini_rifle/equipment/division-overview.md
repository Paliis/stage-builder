## What this is

**Chapter 5** **IPSC Mini Rifle** defines **junior** divisions and **equipment** caps suited to **Mini Rifle** competition. **Age** / **class** flags interact with **Ch.5** — follow **Mini Rifle** PDF, not **full Rifle** assumptions without checking. **C225** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Verify** **registration** vs **visible** rifle — **coach** pressure is **not** a rule change.
2. **Escalate** borderline builds to **RM** / **MD** armoury.
3. **No** disqualifying juniors from a division without **authorised** process.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.5 Mini Rifle**; compare **full Rifle** **Ch.5** only where **MD** explicitly aligns them.
