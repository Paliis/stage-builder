## What this is

**5.1–5.8 Mini Rifle** limits **junior** rifles and **ammunition** to **safe**, **division-legal** configurations — often stricter or different from **full Rifle**. **C240** handgun; verify **Mini Rifle** PDF, not **handgun** or **full Rifle** memos alone.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Watch** for **adult** parts or **calibres** slipped into **Mini** divisions wrongly.
2. **Stop** **unsafe** ammo or damaged guns — **RM**.
3. **Parents** cannot override **chrono** / **MD** ammo policy.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **5.1–5.8 Mini Rifle**; compare **full Rifle** **5.x** only when **MD** says they align.
