## What this is

**5.4** requires competitors (and often staff in firing areas) to wear **eye protection** and **hearing protection** per the **Mini Rifle** PDF: eyewear coverage (side shields), muffs vs plugs, etc. This is **safety**; breaches may stop shooting or yield **DQ** — depending on rule wording and **MD**. **C255** = **Handgun** — use the **Mini Rifle** rulebook for **5.4** text (do not mix with **full Rifle** or **handgun** wording without checking).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Check** before the start that the competitor wears **glasses** and **muffs/plugs** as the match requires.
2. **Stop** prep if protection is removed on the line while others are actively shooting nearby.
3. **Wear your own** protection in hot zones — lead by example.
4. **Remind** spectators / coaches entering risk areas per range policy.
5. **Escalate** to **RM** if a competitor refuses protection after warning.

FPSU often insists on **closed** eyewear; follow the briefing. **`fpsu_delta_verified`**: **false** until **RO_HELPER_V0 §4.1** review.

## Common mistakes

- **Starting without glasses** “for a second” while adjusting gear.
- **Sunglasses** without ballistic rating if the rules forbid them.
- **RO on the line** without personal protection.
- Using **Handgun** or **full Rifle** **5.4** **without** opening the **Mini Rifle** PDF.
