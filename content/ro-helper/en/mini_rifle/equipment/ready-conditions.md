## What this is

**8.1–8.3 Mini Rifle** applies the same **structured** **ready** and **command** framework as **full Rifle**, scaled to **junior** equipment. **Coaches** do not substitute for **RO** commands. **C230** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Use** **Mini Rifle** **Ch.8** text — **not** a relaxed “kids’ rules” command set.
2. **Extra** patience on **understanding** — **zero** tolerance on **unsafe** muzzle / trigger discipline.
3. **Parents** may not **shout** over the **RO** sequence.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **8.1–8.3 Mini Rifle**; compare **full Rifle** **8.1–8.3** if **MD** runs shared procedures.
