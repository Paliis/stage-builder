## What this is

**4.7** covers when a competitor may **reshoot** due to **range equipment** failure or related cases **without** competitor fault. It is **not** a second chance after a **COF** skill error and not an automatic right; **RM/MD** usually define procedure and caps on reshoot counts. **C300** = **Handgun** — use the **Mini Rifle** PDF for **4.7** (do not assume **full Rifle** or **handgun** text without checking).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record facts:** what broke; whether it affected the string; whether it was the same for everyone in the squad.
2. **Notify RM/CRO** immediately; do not promise a reshoot alone.
3. **Preserve** scoresheet / time pending decision if match SOP requires.
4. **After RM approval** — run the reshoot under the same **WSB** conditions if the PDF says so.
5. **Do not use** 4.7 as cover for a **practice** run.

## Common mistakes

- **RO declares a reshoot** without **RM**.
- **Confusing** with reshoots for **gun malfunction** (often **Ch.5** / other clauses).
- **Different treatment** for “favourites” vs the rest of the squad.
- Using **Handgun** or **full Rifle** **4.7** **without** opening the **Mini Rifle** PDF.
