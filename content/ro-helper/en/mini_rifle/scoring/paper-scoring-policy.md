## What this is

**9.5** **IPSC Mini Rifle** defines **paper** scoring for **Mini Rifle** matches. Junior athletes do **not** get relaxed zone rules — **9.5** applies the same way; **C175** handgun; compare with **C177** (full **Rifle**) editorially.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Patience** with height / reach — still **score** per **9.5**, not “helpful” guesses.
2. **Parents** may not lean over the target to “call” a hole — **RO + tools** only.
3. **Record** clearly so **scoring** and coaches see **consistent** calls across squads.

## Common mistakes

- **Softening** zone boundaries “for kids” — **9.5** does not.
- **Letting** a non-RO **touch** the paper before scoring is complete.
