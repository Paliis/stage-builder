## What this is

**9.10 Mini Rifle** applies **full** **official-time** rules to **junior** strings — same **timer** integrity as adults. **C215** handgun; **height** / **reach** may require **RO** to **read** the display for the athlete when **MD** allows.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Ensure** juniors **do not** adjust the timer against rules — **coach** interference is an **RM** issue.
2. **Call** **9.10** faults clearly; **reshoot** only per **Mini Rifle** + **MD**.
3. **Document** weird reads — **no** “average of two displays.”

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.10 Mini Rifle**; verify against **full Rifle** **9.10** if **MD** uses one timer SOP.
