## What this is

**9.9 Mini Rifle** applies **full** **9.9** scoring rules to **junior** special targets. Height and coaching noise do **not** change **miss** / **activation** logic — **Mini Rifle** PDF. **C210** handgun; compare **C212** (full **Rifle**) if both run together.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Extra** clarity on **WSB** for juniors — still **9.9**-accurate scoring.
2. **Parents** do not “call” activations — **RO** + **RM** only.
3. **Hardware** issues — **RM** before more juniors shoot a broken stage.
