## What this is

The **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** rulebook contains a **popper calibration challenge** procedure in its **target / scoring appendices** (the planning matrix references **Handgun Appendix C1** as a parallel — **find the correct appendix in the Mini Rifle PDF**). It governs disputes about **popper** response vs **calibration**, **RM** involvement, evidence, and any **test shot** rules. **Mini Rifle** energy levels and stage design differ from **handgun** — apply **Mini Rifle** text only.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Stop** on-bay arguments; record **facts** (popper position, hits, timer) and direct the competitor to the verified **Mini Rifle** appendix path.
2. **Preserve the stage** until **RM** / **CRO** authorises changes.
3. **Do not** run informal calibration shots without **RM**.
4. **Stay neutral** if **RM** interviews you.
5. **On junior-heavy squads**, still follow the **same** appendix process — do not “shortcut” disputes.

## Common mistakes

- **Using Handgun C1** steps verbatim on a **Mini Rifle** stage.
- **Resetting steel** before **RM** review.
- **Assuming** appendix letters match **PCC** or **Rifle** without opening **Mini Rifle**.
