## What this is

**9.6 Mini Rifle** applies **full** **challenge** and **verification** rules to **junior** matches — **no** informal shortcuts. **Parents** follow the same **RM** process as adults. **C195** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Explain** calmly: **where**, **when**, **how** to protest per **9.6** / **MD**.
2. **Protect** the athlete from **sideline pressure** — official process only.
3. **Preserve** targets and records; **escalate** if a coach blocks **RM** access.
