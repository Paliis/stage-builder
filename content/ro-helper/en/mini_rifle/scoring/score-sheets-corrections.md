## What this is

**9.7 Mini Rifle** applies **full** **scoresheet** integrity rules to **junior** events — **parents** do not initial corrections for the athlete unless **MD** allows **guardian** co-signing. **C200** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Ensure** the **competitor** (and **RO**) sign where **9.7** / **MD** require.
2. **No** “quick fixes” on the line — proper **strike-through** + **initials** + witnesses.
3. **Escalate** if a coach demands a change without **RM**/scoring approval.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.7 Mini Rifle**; compare **full Rifle** **9.7** if **MD** uses one form for both.
