## What this is

**9.3** **IPSC Mini Rifle** applies **tie-breaking** rules to **Mini Rifle** standings. **Junior** categories follow the **same** **9.3** framework — fairness does not mean improvised line decisions. **C165** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Send** tie questions to **scoring** — do not comfort-rank juniors verbally.
2. **Parents** demanding instant resolution — **MD** / official results only.
3. **Clean data** on every stage so **9.3** can run on real numbers.
