## What this is

**9.11 Mini Rifle** applies **software** rules to **junior** matches — same **9.11** discipline as seniors. **Parents** do not get **admin** passwords to “fix” scores. **C220** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Use** the **Mini Rifle** / **MD**-configured **profile** — **not** a borrowed **adult** card by mistake.
2. **Report** wrong **class** flags to **scoring** — **no** ad-hoc edits for “kindness.”
