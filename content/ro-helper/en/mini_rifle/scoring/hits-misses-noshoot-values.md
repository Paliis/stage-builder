## What this is

**9.4** **IPSC Mini Rifle** sets the **value** of hits, misses, and no-shoot penalties for **Mini Rifle** matches. Junior-friendly gear does not change the rule: **PDF + appendices** are authoritative. **C170** = handgun; compare editorially with **C172** (Rifle) when both run at an event.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Same discipline rigor** as adult rifle — accurate calls, no “easy” no-shoot omissions.
2. **Coaches / parents:** may observe, but **9.4** application stays with **RO + scoring** per match policy.
3. **Log everything** the **WSB** requires so **9.4** can be applied in software consistently.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)


### 9.4.1–9.4.2 — point table (Major/Minor)

Verify the numbers in your discipline PDF; for **Jan 2026** (IPSC) the typical table is:

| | A | C | D | Miss | No-Shoot |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| **Major** | 5 | 4 | 2 | -10 | -10 |
| **Minor** | 5 | 3 | 1 | -10 | -10 |

- **9.4 Mini Rifle** — cross-check numbers against **Mini Rifle** appendices, not generic rifle memos.

## Common mistakes

- **Assuming** Mini Rifle uses **identical** numbers to full **Rifle** without opening **9.4** in the Mini book.
- **Soft-pedaling no-shoot** penalties for juniors — rules still apply.
