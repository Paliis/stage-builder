## What this is

**IPSC Mini Rifle** uses the same **conceptual** **A / C / D** + **Major / Minor** framework as other IPSC rifle sports, but **zone maps** and tables are in the **Mini Rifle Jan 2026** book. **C180** handgun; compare **C182** (full **Rifle**) when both run at an event — **appendices** may differ.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **No** “easier” zone for juniors — **9.5 Mini Rifle** is the rule.
2. **Check** the **printed** target matches the **WSB** model.
3. **Record** zones legibly — coaches and parents review these sheets closely.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.5 Mini Rifle** tables + **9.4 Mini Rifle**.
