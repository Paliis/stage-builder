## What this is

**9.2** **IPSC Mini Rifle** defines **scoring methods** for **Mini Rifle** divisions. **Junior** matches still need **correct** method registration per **WSB** — **9.2** applies the same way in principle; details are in the **Mini Rifle** PDF. **C160** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Double-check** with **MD** if a stage mixes **adult** and **junior** squads — method must stay consistent.
2. **Complete** youth-friendly fields if the software tracks them — raw **time** and **hits** remain primary.
3. **No** “easier math” without **RM** approval.
