## What this is

**9.1** **IPSC Mini Rifle** applies **approach / touch** rules to this division. Juniors may need **clear** verbal cues on when they may go downrange — **9.1** still governs **integrity**. **C155** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Reinforce** “wait for the command” after the string if the squad is inexperienced.
2. **Log** any early approach the same as for adults — **fairness** across squads.
3. **Coach / parent** interference with targets — escalate per **MD**, may touch **10.6** if applicable.
