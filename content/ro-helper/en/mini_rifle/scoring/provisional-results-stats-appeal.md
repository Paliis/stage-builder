## What this is

**9.8 Mini Rifle** applies **full** **provisional** / **final** timing rules to **junior** events. **Parents** should read **MD** bulletins — **RO** does not adjudicate placements from a phone app. **C205** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Direct** to **official** results — **no** “you’re winning” from memory.
2. **Keep** **stage** paperwork tight so **kids’** provisionals are fair.
3. **Escalate** if adults argue **9.8** deadlines — **RM** only.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.8 Mini Rifle**; verify combined **stats** events against **full Rifle** **MD** if shared.
