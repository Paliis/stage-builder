## What this is

**Chapter 5** and **division appendices** govern **Shotgun** feeding and **loading**: tube / magazine rules where applicable, **loaders**, **caddies**, shell carriers, and any **capacity** limits for the division. The **RO** may spot obvious illegal extensions or extra loading gear vs **division**; exact calls use **MD** briefing + **RM**. **C250** = **Handgun** — **Shotgun** **Chapter 5** is not pistol magazine rules.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Compare** the gun and loading gear to what the **division** allows (read **Shotgun** PDF + appendix).
2. **Watch** shell carrier / loader count during **equipment check** or pre-start if **MD** requires it.
3. **Do not allow** obviously banned extensions (tube / magazine) or prohibited loader types if the division excludes them.
4. **During the COF**, a fumbled load or dropped shell may trigger **other** rules (**10.5.x**) — do not confuse with division capacity rules.
5. **Escalate** disputed equipment to **RM**.

## Common mistakes

- **Treating** shotgun **Chapter 5** like **handgun** magazine length rules.
- **Confusing** allowed on-body shell count with **WSB** minimum round count.
- **RO** declaring hardware illegal without **RM** process.
