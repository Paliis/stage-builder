## What this is

**8.1–8.3 Shotgun** define **ready** conditions and **RO** **commands** for **shotguns** — **open bolt** / **chamber** / **load** commands per **Shotgun** **Ch.8**, **not** pistol or rifle defaults. **C230** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Follow** **Shotgun** **command** order exactly — **squad** safety depends on predictability.
2. **Control** **muzzle** during **load** / **make ready** — **high** consequence if wrong.
3. **Finish** with **8.3** **unload** / **clear** before **downrange** movement.
