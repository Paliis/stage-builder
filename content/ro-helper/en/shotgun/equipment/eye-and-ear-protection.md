## What this is

**5.4** requires competitors (and often staff in firing areas) to wear **eye protection** and **hearing protection** per the **Shotgun** PDF: eyewear coverage (side shields), muffs vs plugs, etc. Shotgun reports are loud at close range — **hearing protection** is non-negotiable in hot zones. This is **safety**; breaches may stop shooting or yield **DQ** — depending on rule wording and **MD**. **C255** = **Handgun** — use the **Shotgun** rulebook for **5.4** text.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Check** before the start that the competitor wears **glasses** and **muffs/plugs** as the match requires.
2. **Stop** prep if protection is removed on the line while others are actively shooting nearby.
3. **Wear your own** protection in hot zones — lead by example.
4. **Remind** spectators / coaches entering risk areas per range policy.
5. **Escalate** to **RM** if a competitor refuses protection after warning.

## Common mistakes

- **Starting without glasses** “for a second” while adjusting gear.
- **Sunglasses** without ballistic rating if the rules forbid them.
- **RO on the line** without personal protection.
- Reading **Handgun 5.4** **without** opening the **Shotgun** PDF.
