## What this is

**5.1–5.8 Shotgun** covers **shotgun** configuration, **magazine** / **tube** rules, **shell** types, and any **chrono** / **power** language **Shotgun** uses — **Shotgun Jan 2026** only. **C240** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Reject** obvious **gauge** / **shell** mismatches vs **division** / **MD**.
2. **Stop** damaged **actions** or **barrels** — **RM**.
3. **Range-specific** shot rules (steel, minimum dram) — **MD** + **Ch.5**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **5.1–5.8 Shotgun**; unsafe condition → **10.4** / **10.5**.
