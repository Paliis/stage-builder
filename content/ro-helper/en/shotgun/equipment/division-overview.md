## What this is

**Chapter 5** **IPSC Shotgun** defines **Shotgun** **divisions** and **equipment** (gauge, mag tube, optic classes, etc. — **Shotgun Jan 2026**). **RO** handles **visible** compliance; **MD** may add local division availability. **C225** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Check** gun configuration vs **registered** **Shotgun** division per **Ch.5**.
2. **Call** **RM** on **clear** rule breaches — especially **magazine** / **mod** issues.
3. **Do not** apply **pistol** division names or limits to **shotguns**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.5 Shotgun** + appendices referenced in the rulebook.
