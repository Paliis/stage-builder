## What this is

**10.5.1** in **IPSC Shotgun** is the **residual** clause for **unsafe firearm handling** not better covered by **10.4** or a **specific** **10.5** sub-rule (e.g. **muzzle angles**, **drops**, **trigger discipline**, loading rules). Fast loading and port work create edge cases — still **PDF-first**. **C125** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **STOP** if the shotgun is handled in a way that risks people or uncontrolled muzzle movement.
2. **Sort the rule:** **AD** (**10.4**), **180°** (**10.5.2**), **drop** (**10.5.3**), **finger** (**10.5.9–11**) before **10.5.1**.
3. **Describe** what was unsafe (one-handed carry, uncontrolled port exit) in neutral terms tied to the book.
4. **RM** for **DQ** decisions on contested calls.
