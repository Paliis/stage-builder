## What this is

**Chapter 2** **IPSC Shotgun** defines **range** layout, **Safety Area**, and rules for shotguns, ammunition, and shells **off** the **COF**. Shell handling near **loading** tables and squad paths needs **Ch.2** + **MD** map — **C145** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Know** shell / equipment zones vs **hot** bays per briefing.
2. **Stop** ammunition or gun handling in **forbidden** areas — cite **Ch.2** and linked **Ch.10** rules.
3. **Coordinate** with **CRO** on crowded shotgun ranges — flow prevents zone drift.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.2 Shotgun** full read; verify interaction with loading / handling rules in later chapters per edition.
