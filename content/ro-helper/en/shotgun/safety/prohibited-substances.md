## What this is

**10.7** **IPSC Shotgun** sets **prohibited substances** rules for **Shotgun** matches. High squad tempo is **not** an excuse to ignore impairment — **10.7** and **RM** process still apply. **C135** = handgun; use **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** PDF on shotgun courses.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **RM/MD** own investigation and **DQ** declaration per **10.3** / **10.7**.
2. **Stop loading / shooting** if the competitor cannot safely control the **shotgun** and the **berm** sector.
3. **Factual log** for **RM**: stage, time, behaviour cues (no diagnostic claims).

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.7 Shotgun** + **10.3**; separate from **10.6** if the PDF distinguishes grounds.
