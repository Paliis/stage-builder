## What this is

**Section 10.3** of **IPSC Shotgun** covers **general** **DQ** administration: declaration, effect, and linkage to **Chapter 10** grounds (and other chapters if cited). Squad and stage complexity do not change **10.3** — only the **Shotgun** PDF does. **C130** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Anchor** the DQ in a **specific** rule plus **10.3** procedure.
2. **Safe shotgun** after the call — open action / clear per **MD** and **WSB**.
3. **Stats / squad flow** — DQ removes the competitor from further **COF** per **10.3** and event rules.
4. **RM** for borderline process questions.
