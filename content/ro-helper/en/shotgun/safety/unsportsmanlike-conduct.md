## What this is

**10.6** **IPSC Shotgun** covers **unsportsmanlike** behaviour on **Shotgun** courses. Squad pressure and loud bays do not change **10.6** — **RM** still decides serious sanctions. **C140** = handgun; open **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** for the operative text.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Document** interference with scoring, loaders, or other competitors’ **COF**.
2. **RM** for **10.6** **DQ** — RO supplies **witnesses** and **timeline**.
3. **Keep order** without personal feuds — escalate up the chain.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.6 Shotgun** + **10.3**; distinguish from **10.7** if facts are substance-related.
