## What this is

**9.9 Shotgun** addresses **special** presentations — **moving**, **disappearing**, or timed exposures relevant to **Shotgun** **COFs**. Mechanics differ from pistol sports; **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** + **WSB** define hits, misses, and **activator** chains. **C210** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Follow** **WSB** for **throw** / **presentation** order and **scoring** method.
2. **Observe** from **safe** **squad** positions; **thrower** / **trap** issues go to **RM**.
3. **Score** per **9.9 Shotgun** — not handgun **moving-target** habits.
4. **Squad flow:** ensure **everyone** understands when a **special** target is **live**.
