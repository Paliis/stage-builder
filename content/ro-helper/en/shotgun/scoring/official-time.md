## What this is

**9.10 Shotgun** defines **official time** for **Shotgun** strings — often **squad** flow with **multiple** engagements per run. Timer **placement** and **stop** commands follow **Shotgun** **WSB** + **Jan 2026** PDF. **C215** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Apply** **9.10** after each **valid** **COF** segment the **WSB** times.
2. **Shotgun** blast / **movement** — protect timer per **MD**; **readable** capture every time.
3. **Faults** — **RM** path; **no** squad pressure to accept a bad read.
