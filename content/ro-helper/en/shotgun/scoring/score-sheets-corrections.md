## What this is

**9.7 Shotgun** defines **scoresheet** handling for **Shotgun** — often **squad-level** timing and **hit** grids. **C200** handgun; **correction** rules must come from **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** + **MD** tablet SOP.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Complete** **hits** / **misses** / **penalties** fields the **WSB** and software require.
2. **Corrections** only with **auditable** marks — **no** silent database edits if **MD** forbids them.
3. **After squad** leaves, follow **9.7** + **scoring** policy for **locked** results.
