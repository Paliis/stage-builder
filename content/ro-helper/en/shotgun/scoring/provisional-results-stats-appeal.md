## What this is

**9.8 Shotgun** covers **provisional** postings and **finalisation** for **Shotgun** matches — often **high stage counts** and **squad** timing imports. **RO** supplies **clean** per-stage data; **9.8** + **MD** define challenge windows. **C205** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Refer** to **official** **9.8** / **MD** channels — **no** squad-bay ranking promises.
2. **Ensure** **squad** sheets sync with **scoring** before errors propagate into **provisional** tables.
3. **Do not** restart arguments about **placements** after deadlines — **RM** only.
