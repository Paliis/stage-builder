## What this is

**9.1** **IPSC Shotgun** controls **when** competitors may **approach** plates, paper, clays, or other **targets** and what counts as **touch** / early movement. Squad flow is fast — **RO** attention after the last shot matters. **C155** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Hold** competitors behind the line until **authorized** approach per **Ch.8–9** and **WSB**.
2. **Note** if steel was **reset** or paper **handled** without permission — affects **9.1** and score validity.
3. **RM** if the breach type is unclear in the **Shotgun** book.
