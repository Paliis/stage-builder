## What this is

**9.11 Shotgun** addresses **scoring software** for **Shotgun** — **squad** timing, **hit** grids, **penalty** stacks per **Chapter 9 Shotgun**. **C220** handgun; **RO** must follow **Shotgun** **field** semantics in the app.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Enter** **squad** / **stage** IDs exactly — **mis-keys** break **exports**.
2. **No** manual override of **program** totals — **chief scoring** only.
3. **Connectivity** loss — **MD** paper + later **import** per **9.11** workflow.
