## What this is

**9.2** **IPSC Shotgun** defines **scoring methods** for **Shotgun** matches (including how hits, penalties, and time combine — **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** PDF). **RO** supplies **clean** stage data: **WSB** method, times, misses, procedurals per rules. **C160** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Align** each stage with **9.2** and **WSB** before squadding shoots it wrong.
2. **Record** steel / paper results per **MD**; **no** retroactive method change without **RM**.
3. **Squad discipline** — one method slip ruins **stats** for everyone.
