## What this is

**9.6 Shotgun** covers **verification** and **challenges** for **Shotgun** scoring — including **steel** + **paper** **COFs** and squad **timing** issues. **C195** handgun; follow **Shotgun** **Jan 2026** for deadlines and filing rules.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Hold** targets / stage props per **RM** when a **9.6** review is open.
2. **Record** squad-level facts (order of engagement, misses) **neutrally** for **RM**.
3. **Do not** re-score from memory after teardown — **evidence** first.
