## What this is

**9.3** **IPSC Shotgun** covers **tie-breaking** for **Shotgun** aggregate scores. High stage counts make ties common — **9.3** still belongs to **match administration**, not **RO** improvisation. **C165** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Ensure** each stage’s **raw** results are complete — ties are resolved from **good** inputs.
2. **No** “sudden death” on the bay unless **MD** + **9.3** authorize it.
3. **Separate** tie process from **protests** (**9.6**) — different path.
