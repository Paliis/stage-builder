## What this is

**Creeping** is prohibited **movement** or **lean** of body or **shotgun** **before** the start signal that improves readiness for the first shot or first engagement motion. **Shotgun Jan 2026** covers this under **10.2.6** (confirm exact wording in your PDF). Ready posture (mount, muzzle discipline, foot box) differs from handgun — apply **Shotgun** **Chapter 10** only.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Observe** the ready area, **Stand by** → **Start** sequence, and any **WSB** limits on stance or gun position.
2. **Record** pre-start foot shift, body lean, or gun movement toward the first target or loading position.
3. **Apply** **10.2.6** after PDF verification; involve **CRO** / **RM** on edge cases.
4. **Brief briefly** what was seen; protests per **9.6** where applicable.
5. **Document** command timing and stage reference.

## Common mistakes

- **Judging shotgun** creeping with **handgun** body cues only.
- **Calling creeping** on normal balance or breathing if the PDF does not support it.
- **Confusing** creeping with **loading / gun ready** faults under other **10.2** or safety rules — read the right clause.
