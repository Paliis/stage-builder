## What this is

**Significant advantage** is an **unfair material gain** during the **COF**—for example shooting from a position that improves engagement on paper, steel, or mixed arrays beyond **WSB** intent. **Shotgun Jan 2026** anchors this under **10.2.1.1** and **10.2.2**. **Shotgun** movement and port geometry differ from handgun — adjudicate from **Shotgun** **Chapter 10** only.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** competitor position vs **WSB** start boxes and array boundaries.
2. **Compare** to fair execution; escalate to **CRO** / **RM** if advantage vs simple foot fault is debatable.
3. **Apply** **10.2.1.1** / **10.2.2** per PDF — do not relabel as generic **10.1** when advantage clauses govern.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **Note** which **array** the advantage occurred on for multi-bay stages.
