## What this is

**10.2.12** in **IPSC Shotgun** addresses firing when the **ammunition type** (e.g. slug vs buck vs bird, pellet size, or other categories **as defined in the PDF**) is **not permitted** for that **COF**, division, or match ammunition rules. This card is **shotgun-only** — do not apply to other disciplines. Exact definitions, **procedural** count, **DQ** thresholds (if any), and relation to **WSB** / match addenda are **only** from the **Jan 2026 Shotgun** PDF.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Confirm facts:** what was loaded / fired; what the **WSB** and match bulletin allow for that stage and division.
2. **Read 10.2.12** in context (may tie to **Ch.5** ammunition rules — follow cross-references in the PDF).
3. **Apply the sanction** strictly per **10.2.12** after verification — do not guess from sound or hole pattern alone if the rulebook requires evidence.
4. **Scoresheet** entry: stage, shot sequence, and stated ammo finding (per match evidence rules).
5. **Escalate to RM** if division or bulletin text is unclear vs the rulebook.

## Common mistakes

- **Calling “wrong ammo”** without a rule and **WSB** basis.
- **Mixing disciplines:** **10.2.12** is for **Shotgun** — not **rifle** / **PCC** matrices.
- **Ignoring match bulletin:** some limits are match-level, not only generic IPSC text.
