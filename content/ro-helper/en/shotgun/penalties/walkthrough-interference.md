## What this is

During the **group inspection** / **walkthrough**, **special** limits apply: what may be done with the **shotgun** and body, how positions and lines of fire may be tested. **8.7.2** and **8.7.3** in **Shotgun Jan 2026** cover different aspects of this phase (do not merge them with **8.7.1** — see card **C70**). Outcomes are **only** from the **Shotgun** PDF — not **Handgun** **Chapter 8** alone.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Run the walkthrough** per match briefing and **Section 8.7**: clear instructions to the group, RO positioning.
2. **Stop violations** immediately (words, gestures, prohibited testing) — reduce “facts on the ground” before the match.
3. **Apply sanctions** under the correct sub-rule (**8.7.2** / **8.7.3**) after PDF verification; log per match procedure.
4. **Repeat or systemic issues** — **CRO/RM**.
5. **Do not auto-map** to **Chapter 10** **DQ** if the PDF keeps the case under **8.7** procedurals.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **8.7** as a whole in the **Shotgun** PDF.

## Common mistakes

- **One penalty for everything:** **8.7.2** vs **8.7.3** — different situations in the PDF.
- **Late reaction:** tolerating prohibited testing during walkthrough — hard to restore fairness later.
- **Overlap with C70:** keep **8.7.1** vs **8.7.2/8.7.3** phases distinct.
