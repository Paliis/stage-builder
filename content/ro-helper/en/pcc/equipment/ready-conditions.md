## What this is

**8.1–8.3 PCC** define **ready conditions** for the **carbine** (bolt / chamber / magazine / sling or carry per **PCC** **Ch.8**) and the **RO** **command** flow around the **COF**. **C230** = **Handgun** — **PCC** **Chapter 8** is authoritative for PCC lines.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Use** the **official** command set and order from **PCC** **8.1–8.3** (English or **MD** translation).
2. **Verify** **muzzle**, **finger**, **mechanical** state before **Start** — **PCC** recoil and **size** need **space** discipline.
3. **Stop** illegal ready states; repeat — **RM**.
4. **After** **COF** — **unload** / **show clear** per **8.3** and **Ch.9** approach rules.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **8.1**, **8.2**, **8.3 PCC**; do not substitute **Handgun** wording without checking **PCC** PDF.
