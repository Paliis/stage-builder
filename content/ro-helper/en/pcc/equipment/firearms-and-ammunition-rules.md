## What this is

**5.1–5.8 PCC** govern **carbine** configuration, **allowed** modifications, **magazines**, and **ammunition** (including **PF** / chrono rules in the same chapter structure). **C240** = **Handgun** — **PCC** **Chapter 5** is separate; do not apply pistol barrel / holster rules to **PCC**.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Spot** obvious **calibre** / **magazine** mismatches vs **division**.
2. **Stop** on **unsafe** damage (cracked lower, bad bolt) — **RM**.
3. **Direct** **PF** questions to **chrono** — **no** improvised velocity guesses.
4. **Ammo** type must match **range** **MD** (steel, frangible — local add-ons).

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **5.1–5.8 PCC** as one block; **10.4** / **10.5** if unsafe.
