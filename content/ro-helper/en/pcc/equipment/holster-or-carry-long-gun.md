## What this is

**PCC** carry rules live in **Chapter 5 PCC** and division **appendices**: **sling** types, **muzzle** orientation when slung, **retention**, and transitions to **ready** — **not** handgun **holster** rules. **C240** covers **handgun** holsters only.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Spot-check** **sling** / **carry** setup vs **division** limits (single-point vs two-point if regulated — **PDF**).
2. **Stop** unsafe **muzzle** direction while moving between props with a **slung** carbine.
3. **Escalate** borderline gear to **RM** — **no** improvised rigs that bypass **Ch.5**.
4. **Equipment changes** mid-match may need **re-verification** per **MD**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.5 PCC** + appendix; align with **8.1** when moving from **carry** to **ready**.
