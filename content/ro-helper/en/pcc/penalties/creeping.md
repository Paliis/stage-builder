## What this is

**Creeping** is prohibited **movement** or **lean** before the start signal that improves readiness for the first shot. **PCC Jan 2026** covers this under **10.2.6**. **PCC** ready positions (stock mount, muzzle height, foot placement near ports) differ from **handgun** — apply **PCC** **Chapter 10**, not another book.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Observe** ready posture through **Stand by** → **Start** per SOP and **WSB**.
2. **Record** pre-start shifts of feet, torso, or carbine.
3. **Apply** **10.2.6** after PDF verification; involve **CRO** / **RM** on edge cases.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **On mixed bays**, confirm discipline before citing **10.2.6**.
