## What this is

A **foot fault** is **out-of-area ground contact** or **fault-line** breach during the **COF** contrary to the **WSB** or general line rules. **PCC Jan 2026** anchors this under **10.2.1** (see **10.2.1.1** in your PDF for significant advantage while faulting). **PCC** combines **long gun** handling with often **tight** fault boxes — read **PCC**, not **Rifle** or **Handgun**, when adjudicating.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** line identity, contact evidence, timing vs shots for **10.2.1.1** analysis.
2. **Confirm WSB** activation of the line for that position / movement.
3. **Apply** **10.2.1** / **10.2.1.1** on the scoresheet per PDF — avoid mis-bucketing as **10.1**.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **On mixed bays**, state which discipline’s **10.2.1** text you are using.

## Common mistakes

- **Rifle** memory anchors on a **PCC**-only match (or vice versa) without opening the right PDF.
- **Defaulting to 10.1** when **10.2.1** clearly applies.
- **Unclear scoresheet** — no shot index or line name.
