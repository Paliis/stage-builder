## What this is

A **mandatory reload** in the **WSB** means the competitor **must** perform a reload exactly as written (type, **COF** point, count, etc.). Failure to meet that requirement is anchored under **10.2.4** in **IPSC PCC** — conditions and **procedural** count **only** from the **Jan 2026** PDF. **PCC** reloads differ from **handgun** in handling and ports — still cite **PCC** **Chapter 10**, not another book.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Read the WSB:** mandatory reload location(s); exceptions if any.
2. **Record facts:** reload performed or not; matches WSB (tactical vs administrative — **per WSB and rules text only**).
3. **Apply** **10.2.4** after PDF check; avoid mis-bucket under **10.2.2** when the issue is reload-only.
4. **Brief the competitor** briefly; **9.6** for challenges.
5. **Document** WSB reference + **COF** moment.
