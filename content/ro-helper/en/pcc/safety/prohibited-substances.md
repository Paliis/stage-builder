## What this is

**10.7** **IPSC PCC** prohibits being under the influence of **substances** listed in the **Jan 2026** **PCC** PDF (typically alcohol and drugs — **read the book**) on the range and/or while competing. Consequences are **usually DQ**; who is covered and how testing works are **PDF + match regs** only. **C135** = **Handgun** — use **PCC** at PCC matches.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Escalate** suspicion to **RM/MD** — do not act as investigator or medic.
2. **Stop the COF** if impairment creates **immediate** safety risk with a long gun.
3. **Objective notes:** observations, time, stage — no amateur diagnoses.
4. **RM** for **10.7** decisions, **DQ** paperwork, and any testing policy the MD publishes.
5. **Privacy / law** — follow local rules and federation policy.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.7 PCC** + **10.3** (DQ process); keep **10.6** separate if the PDF does.
