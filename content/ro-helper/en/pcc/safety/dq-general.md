## What this is

**Section 10.3** of **IPSC PCC** states **general** rules on **disqualification (DQ)**: how it is declared, its effect, and how it ties to the **specific** DQ grounds in **10.4**, **10.5**, **10.7**, etc. **10.3** is the **administrative** frame; the **substantive** breach is always another rule. **C130** = **Handgun** — use **PCC** PDF on PCC matches.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Cite the operative rule** (e.g. **10.4**, **10.5.x**) **and** follow **10.3** for process.
2. **Secure the line**; long-gun handling after a DQ call may need **RM** direction.
3. **Inform** the competitor per **10.3** and the **match book**.
4. **Scoresheet / stats** — DQ recorded for the correct **division** and **stage**.
5. **Challenges** — per match rules (**9.6** where applicable), not ad hoc arguments on the bay.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **10.3 PCC** with the **Chapter 10** rule that triggered the DQ.
- Confirm whether DQ ends **all** PCC participation at the event or only that division — **PDF + MD**.
