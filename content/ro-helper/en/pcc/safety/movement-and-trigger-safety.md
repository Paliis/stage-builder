## What this is

**8.5** **IPSC PCC** covers **movement** with the carbine during the **COF** (body, muzzle discipline, transitions — **PDF**). It works alongside **10.5.9–10.5.11** on **trigger finger** (**C116**), not **C115** (handgun). Check **both**: movement standard under **8.5**, then whether a **finger** DQ applies under **Ch.10.5**.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Position** to see **muzzle line** and **trigger guard** during footwork and sling changes.
2. **STOP** on clear danger — long-gun arcs are large on tight stages.
3. **Classify:** unsafe **carry / movement** vs **finger in guard** — different paragraphs; verify outcomes in **PCC**.
4. **RM** if sanction text is ambiguous.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **8.5 PCC**; do not merge with **10.5.2** (180°) if the fact pattern is different.
