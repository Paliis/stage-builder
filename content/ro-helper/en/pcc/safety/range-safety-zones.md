## What this is

**Chapter 2** **IPSC PCC** defines **range** organisation: **Safety Area**, where the carbine may be handled, ammunition rules, and movement between zones **off** the **COF**. Layout details are **PCC** **Jan 2026** only — **C145** is **Handgun**; long-gun handling and slings follow **PCC** text.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Learn the MD map:** Safety Area, bays, hot/cold, bag / table rules for **PCC**.
2. **Stop** unsafe zone breaches immediately — cite **Ch.2** + **Ch.10** where they interact.
3. **No informal exceptions** — use **RM** for waivers.
4. **Brief** competitors on where **carbine** and **ammo** may go before they cross lines.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **Ch.2 PCC** end-to-end; cross-check **Ch.10** (e.g. ammunition in Safety Area — confirm numbering in your PDF).
