## What this is

**10.6** **IPSC PCC** defines **unsportsmanlike** conduct for PCC competition (insults, cheating, interference with officials, score issues, etc. — **read the full PDF**). Sanctions are serious and usually require **RM/MD** process — not improvised line justice. **C140** = **Handgun**; use **PCC** **10.6** at PCC matches.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Facts, not verdicts:** who said/did what, when, witnesses — hand to **RM**.
2. **Professional tone:** do not escalate verbally; follow **CRO/RM** removal from the line if the briefing allows.
3. **Separate** from **10.7** (substances) and **Ch.8** assistance rules if the PDF distinguishes them.
4. **Written referral** per **MD** policy.
