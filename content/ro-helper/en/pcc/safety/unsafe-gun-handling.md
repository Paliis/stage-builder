## What this is

**10.5.1** in **IPSC PCC** is the **general** clause for **unsafe firearm handling** when the situation is **not** better described by a **specific** rule in **Section 10.5** or **10.4** (e.g. **AD**, **180°**, **dropped gun**, **trigger finger**). Use **PCC** text — **C125** is **handgun** only. Outcome is usually **DQ**; confirm in **Jan 2026** **PCC** PDF.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Stop the hazard:** cease fire; **muzzle** arc; safe distance around a long gun.
2. **Classify:** match facts to **10.4**, **10.5.2**, **10.5.3**, **10.5.9–11**, etc., before defaulting to **10.5.1**.
3. **If 10.5.1** is the right fit — describe the **act** (e.g. unsafe sling transition) in the log.
4. **RM** confirmation if match regs require it.
5. **No DQ** without a **PDF** hook.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **10.5.1** together with the rest of **Ch.10.5 PCC** — residual clause, not a substitute for specifics.
