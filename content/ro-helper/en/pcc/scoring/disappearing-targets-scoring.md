## What this is

**9.9 PCC** covers scoring for **moving**, **disappearing**, and other **special** targets. Sub-rule numbering and examples may differ from **Handgun** — read **PCC Jan 2026** end-to-end. **C210** = **Handgun** reference only; **WSB** defines **activator** order and **reset** behaviour.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Parse the WSB:** activation sequence, time windows, second-shot rules, **reset**.
2. **Watch** target behaviour from a **safe** observation point; second RO / video per **MD** on complex **COFs**.
3. **Score** per **9.9 PCC** — not **Handgun** memos.
4. **Mechanical failure** — **RM** before more runs.
5. **Disputes** — **9.6**; state **facts** only.
