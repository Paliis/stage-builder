## What this is

**9.7 PCC** governs **scoresheet** completion, **signatures**, and **lawful corrections** — paper or app — and links to **9.6** (challenges). **C200** = **Handgun**; use **PCC** **Jan 2026** for PCC matches.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Complete** mandatory fields per **MD** (stage, division, time, hits, procedurals, RO ID).
2. **Sign** only when required and after **competitor** confirmation where rules say so.
3. **Correct** errors only with the **9.7** pattern (strike-through, initials, **RM**/scoring witness — **confirm** in PDF / briefing).
4. **After protest** — **no** solo edits; follow **RM** decision.
5. **Retain** photos / copies if **MD** mandates audit trail.
