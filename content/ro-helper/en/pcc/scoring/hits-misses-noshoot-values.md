## What this is

**9.4** **IPSC PCC** assigns **point values** (or the match software’s equivalent) to **hits**, **misses**, and **no-shoot** impacts for **PCC** targets. Exact tables and **steel** treatment are in the **Jan 2026 PCC** PDF and **appendices**. **C170** covers **Handgun** — use the **PCC** book for PCC divisions.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Paper:** score per **9.5 PCC** after an allowed approach — **9.4** defines what each zone means.
2. **Steel / reactive:** record knockdown / miss per **COF** and **Chapter 9 PCC**.
3. **No-shoot:** log every no-shoot hit; **9.4** sets the penalty — do not drop it from the string.
4. **No** ad-hoc point values from memory — only this edition’s PDF and **appendices**.
5. **Disputed paper** — **CRO/RM** or formal **9.6** path, not a friendly re-grade.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.4 PCC** with **9.5** (paper) and **9.9** if moving / special targets apply.

## Local (FPSU)

Calibres and target packages must match the **MD** bulletin. **`fpsu_delta_verified`**: **false** until **RO_HELPER_V0 §4.1** review.

## Common mistakes

- **Using handgun zone tables** for a PCC match — open **PCC** **9.4** / appendices.
- **Omitting no-shoot** from the recorded penalties.
- **Confusing miss with FTE** — follow **PCC** **9.5** / **9.5.6** as applicable.
