## What this is

**9.1** **IPSC PCC** sets when a competitor may **approach** or **touch** targets during/after the **COF**, and what breaches **scoring** or triggers **procedural** / other sanctions — **PCC** **Jan 2026** only. **C155** = **Handgun**; do not assume identical timing with pistol rules.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Hold the line** until **Ch.8–9** commands and **WSB** allow movement toward targets.
2. **Stop** premature approach — long guns add **muzzle** sweep risk near paper and steel.
3. **Log** target ID, time, contact vs no contact, for **scoring** and protests.
4. **RM** if **9.1** links to **DQ** in this edition.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.1 PCC** + **Ch.8** finish-of-COF commands.
