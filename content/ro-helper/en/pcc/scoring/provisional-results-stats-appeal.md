## What this is

**9.8 PCC** governs **provisional** results and published **statistics**: publication moment, **challenge** windows, and when results go **final**. Line **RO** does not run the server — but **accurate** scoresheets feed **9.8**. **C205** = **Handgun**.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Send** competitors to the official **MD** / **9.8** channel for standings — **no** bay promises.
2. **Enter** clean **PCC** stage data so provisionals are not corrupted at input.
3. **Report** sheet vs app mismatches to **scoring** — **9.7** + **9.8** path, not rogue edits.
4. **Do not** rush **scoring** to “fix” a place without **RM** process.
