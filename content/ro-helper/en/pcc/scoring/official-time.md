## What this is

**9.10 PCC** defines **official time** for a **PCC** string — typically the **approved** timer read after a valid **COF** — plus **malfunction** handling and **reshoot** conditions per **PCC Jan 2026**. **C215** = **Handgun**; use the **PCC** book for PCC divisions.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Mount** the timer per **WSB** / **MD** (PCC **muzzle** blast and **movement** may differ from handgun placement).
2. **Capture** the display **immediately**; **no** verbal rounding.
3. **Invalid** read / double beep / freeze — **9.10 PCC** + **MD** (reshoot or other remedy).
4. **Log** anomalies for **RM**; **do not** “invent” a time.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.10 PCC**; **reshoot** cross-refs per PDF and **Chapter 4** if cited.
