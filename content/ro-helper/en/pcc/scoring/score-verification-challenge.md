## What this is

**9.6 PCC** sets **verification** of results and **challenges** to scoring: deadlines, **who** may file, evidence, escalation to **RM**. Line **RO** is often first contact — same duties as **Handgun** **C195**, but **PCC Jan 2026** text controls **PCC** matches.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Direct** the competitor to the **9.6** / **MD** protest path — **no** outcome promises.
2. **Preserve** targets, timer, scoresheet, photos — **do not** clear the bay early if **RM** says hold.
3. **No** solo edits to **signed** sheets after a challenge — authorised decision only.
4. **Factual** answers if **RM** asks; **CRO/RM** if behaviour is unsafe or abusive.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.6 PCC**; cross-check **9.7** / **9.8** in the same book when corrections or provisional results apply.
