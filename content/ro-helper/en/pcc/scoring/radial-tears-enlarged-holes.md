## What this is

**9.5.4** **IPSC PCC** covers **radial tears**, **enlarged** holes, and how to tell a **true projectile cut** from **paper damage** when scoring **PCC** paper. Criteria and overlays are in the **PCC Jan 2026** PDF — not the **Handgun** text. **C185** = handgun; **C176** = **PCC** paper overview.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Use** the **PCC**-approved **gauge / overlay** when **MD** requires it for doubtful holes.
2. **Do not** credit a zone by **stretching** torn paper past **9.5.4 PCC**.
3. **Photo / retain** targets per **MD** if a **9.6** challenge is likely.
4. **Escalate** repeated disputes on a stage to **CRO/RM**.
5. **One standard** for all squads on that **COF**.
