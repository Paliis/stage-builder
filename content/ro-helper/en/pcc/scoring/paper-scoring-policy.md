## What this is

**9.5** **IPSC PCC** governs **paper** scoring: **zones**, **boundary lines**, **no-shoot** contacts, **radial tears**, **FTE**, combinations with **steel**, etc. This mirrors **Handgun** logic but must be read in the **PCC** **Jan 2026** PDF and match **appendices**. **C175** = **Handgun** overview.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Approach** paper only after **safe** commands (**9.1**, **Chapter 8 PCC**).
2. **Use** overlays / gauges when **MD** or match rules require them for doubtful holes.
3. **Record** each paper target per **WSB** and scoring software — zones, no-shoots, penalties.
4. **Unresolved** calls — **CRO** / **RM**, not crowd consensus.
5. **Challenges** — **9.6**; no post-signature edits without **9.6** / **RM** process.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- Read **9.5 PCC** with **9.4** (values). Sub-rules like **9.5.4** (paper damage) apply per **PCC** text.
- Handgun deep-dive cards **C180**, **C185**, **C190** follow the same **rule numbering** in **Handgun**; for PCC, use **9.5** in the **PCC** PDF.
