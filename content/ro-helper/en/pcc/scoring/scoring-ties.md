## What this is

**9.3** **IPSC PCC** sets **tie-breaking** when aggregate **PCC** results match (per software / rules). Resolution is **match-level** (**scoring / MD / RM**) — not the line **RO**. **C165** = **Handgun**; use **PCC** **9.3** for PCC divisions.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** accurate **COF** data — time, hits, penalties — so ties can be broken upstream.
2. **Do not** rank tied shooters on the bay or invent shoot-offs unless **9.3** + **MD** allow.
3. **Refer** placement questions to **official results** — same process as **C165**, different PDF.
