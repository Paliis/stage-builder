## What this is

**9.11 PCC** governs **approved** scoring **software** for **PCC** matches — alignment with **Chapter 9 PCC**, exports, and who owns corrections. **RO** inputs must match **PCC** fields (not **Handgun** defaults). **C220** = **Handgun** overview.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Map** time, hits, procedurals, **FTE** into the **PCC** profile your **MD** selected.
2. **No** post-sync edits without **chief scoring** / **RM**.
3. **Bug reports** upstream — **no** shadow spreadsheets on the bay.
4. **Division** / **category** must match **registration** for **PCC** **Major/Minor** math.
