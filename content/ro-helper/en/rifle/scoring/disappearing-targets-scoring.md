## What this is

**9.9 Rifle** governs **special** target systems — **moving**, **disappearing**, poppers that drive chains, etc. — at **Rifle** distances and **barricade** contexts. **C210** handgun; **9.9 Rifle** text is authoritative.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Confirm** **safe** angles before tracking moving targets — **muzzle** discipline with long guns.
2. **Execute** **WSB** activation and **scoring** lines exactly; **multi-position** stages may repeat **9.9** logic per array.
3. **Stop** on **mechanical** faults — **RM** call.
4. **No** “static” **miss** count when **9.9** prescribes something else.
