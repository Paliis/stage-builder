## What this is

**9.6 Rifle** governs **score verification** and **challenges** for **Rifle** matches. Multi-position **COFs** and long-range evidence can complicate protests — **9.6** + **MD** define the process. **C195** = handgun overview.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Point** to the correct **RM** / **scoring** desk and **deadlines** per **9.6 Rifle**.
2. **Secure** paper targets, **steel** state (if disputed), **timer** dumps, squad **scoresheets**.
3. **Do not** “fix it on the spot” by re-shooting or re-scoring without **RM** authorisation.
4. **Note** which **position** the competitor fired from if **RM** needs reconstruction.
