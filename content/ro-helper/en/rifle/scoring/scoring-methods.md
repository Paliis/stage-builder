## What this is

**9.2** **IPSC Rifle** lists **scoring methods** for **Rifle** competition. Long-gun matches may use the same **conceptual** families as handgun (per PDF) but **coefficients** and **division** rules can differ — **Rifle** **Jan 2026** is the source. **C160** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Verify** the stage’s method from **WSB** before the first shooter.
2. **Record** hits on paper/steel per **MD** procedure; **procedurals** as defined for **Rifle**.
3. **Escalate** method disputes to **RM** / stats — not the bay argument.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.2 Rifle** with match **appendices** and **division** bulletins.
