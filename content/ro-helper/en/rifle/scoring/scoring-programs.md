## What this is

**9.11 Rifle** covers **scoring software** obligations for **Rifle** — formulas vs **Chapter 9 Rifle**, multi-position **stage** lines, **export** integrity. **C220** handgun; **RO** must use **Rifle**-aware **field** layouts.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Fill** every **position** / **target** column the **WSB** and app require.
2. **Escalate** wrong **hit factor** / **time** mode bugs to **scoring** — **no** manual point hacks.
3. **Offline** mode — paper path per **MD**, then **clean** import.
