## What this is

The **Rifle Jan 2026** rulebook includes a **popper calibration challenge** procedure in its **target / scoring appendices** (the internal matrix uses **Handgun Appendix C1** as a planning parallel — **locate the exact appendix in the Rifle PDF**). It covers disputes about **popper** fall vs **calibration**, evidence, **RM** authority, and any **test shot** rules. **Rifle** stages may use steel differently from **handgun** — still follow **Rifle** text, not another book.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Stop** on-bay debates; record **facts** and point to the **Rifle** appendix procedure once verified.
2. **Preserve the stage** until **RM** / **CRO** clears changes.
3. **Do not** authorise calibration tests yourself.
4. **Give neutral facts** to **RM** if asked.
5. **Do not assume** PCC and **Rifle** appendices are identical — open both only when comparing study notes, not during a live call.
