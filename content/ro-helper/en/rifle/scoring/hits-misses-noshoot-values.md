## What this is

**9.4** **IPSC Rifle** defines how **hits**, **misses**, and **no-shoot** strikes translate into **score** for **Rifle** COFs. Distances, paper zones, and **steel** logic may differ from handgun — always from **Rifle** **Jan 2026** + appendices. **C170** = handgun baseline.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Apply** **9.5 Rifle** for paper measurement; **9.4** tells you what each outcome is worth.
2. **Steel:** record per stage definition (fall / hit zone) — **9.4** ties penalties to those outcomes.
3. **No-shoot:** never “forget” a no-shoot in the penalty column.
4. **Multi-distance** stages: confirm which **target type** and **scoring method** the **WSB** selected before you assign values.
5. **Disputes:** escalate per **9.6**, don’t re-invent **9.4** on the spot.
