## What this is

**9.5.4** **IPSC Rifle** defines how **tears** and **irregular** holes are scored on **Rifle** paper. Higher **velocity** / different **calibres** can change how paper **rips** — still **9.5.4 Rifle** + **appendices**, not handgun habits. **C185** handgun; **C177** = **Rifle** paper policy.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Apply** **Rifle**-specific **gauges** when **MD** mandates them for the match’s **paper** types.
2. **Distance** does not excuse ignoring a **tear** that falls outside **9.5.4**.
3. **Barricade / prone** shots: score the **same** **9.5.4** standard once you **safely** approach the target.
4. **Document** borderline calls — **RM** review if the stage shows a pattern.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.5.4 Rifle**; pair with **9.5** generally (**C177**).
