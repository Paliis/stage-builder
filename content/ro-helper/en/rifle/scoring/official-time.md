## What this is

**9.10 Rifle** sets **official time** rules for **Rifle** **COFs** — timer placement at **distance**, **barricade** shots, and **multi-string** stages. **C215** handgun; **long-gun** handling and **RO** position may differ from pistol bays.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Position** timer and **RO** so the display is **visible** and **safe** after **recoil** / **movement**.
2. **Record** time per string; **confirm** which **string** if the **WSB** splits times.
3. **Timer** fault — **9.10 Rifle** + **MD**; **no** guessed splits.
