## What this is

**9.3** **IPSC Rifle** governs **tie-breaking** for **Rifle** results. **RO** responsibility ends at **accurate** stage data; **who wins** a tie is **scoring / MD** per **9.3**. **C165** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Avoid** “splitting” ties by guess — forward to **stats**.
2. **Preserve** signed scoresheets — no post-hoc tweaks to force an order.
3. **Multi-gun** events: confirm which **discipline** totals use **9.3** — **MD** briefing.
