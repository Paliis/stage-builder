## What this is

**9.1** **IPSC Rifle** governs **approach** and **touch** rules for targets on **Rifle** courses. Barricades and minimum distances can make “leaning in” look like an approach — **9.1** and **WSB** define what is legal. **C155** = handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Watch** the competitor’s path after the last shot — rifles cover distance quickly.
2. **Record** if paper is **taped** / adjusted without authority per **9.1**.
3. **Coordinate** with **scoring** if a target position may have shifted.
