## What this is

**9.7 Rifle** sets **scoresheet** rules for **Rifle** matches — including **multi-position** strings where **extra rows** or **target indices** may appear. **C200** handgun; follow **Rifle** PDF for **9.7** specifics.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** each **position** / **target** the **WSB** requires — **no** blank mandatory cells.
2. **Corrections** with full **audit trail** per **9.7 Rifle** + **MD**.
3. **Do not** swap **times** between strings without **authorised** scoring action.
4. **Link** sheet fixes to **9.6** outcomes when a protest was filed.
