## What this is

**9.5** **IPSC Rifle** covers **paper** target scoring at **varied distances** and target types: **zones**, **lines**, **no-shoot** interactions, **radial tears**, **FTE**, and **steel** combinations. **C175** = handgun; always use **Rifle** **Jan 2026** + appendices.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Confirm** which **paper target** and **scoring method** the **WSB** selected before you score.
2. **Approach** only when allowed; **safety** first (long guns, muzzle discipline).
3. **Measure** doubtful holes with **match-approved** tools when required.
4. **Document** each target clearly for **scoring** — especially on multi-position **COFs**.
5. **Escalate** ambiguous cases to **CRO/RM**; **9.6** for disputes.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.5 Rifle** + **9.4**; compare **9.5.4** and related sub-rules in the **Rifle** book.

## Common mistakes

- **Assuming** handgun zone sizes **apply** to **Rifle** paper without checking **9.5**.
- **Scoring from the wrong distance** or **wrong target face** on a multi-bay stage.
