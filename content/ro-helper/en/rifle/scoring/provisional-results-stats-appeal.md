## What this is

**9.8 Rifle** sets rules for **provisional** leaderboards and **stats** in **Rifle** matches — often **multi-day** or **many stages**. **RO** keeps **stage** inputs accurate; **finals** timing follows **9.8 Rifle** + **MD**. **C205** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Point** athletes to **official** provisional postings — tablets can lag.
2. **Flag** duplicate / wrong **stage** uploads before they propagate to **9.8**.
3. **Never** promise **overall** place from partial **provisionals**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.8 Rifle**; **9.6** / **9.3** / **9.7** as referenced.
