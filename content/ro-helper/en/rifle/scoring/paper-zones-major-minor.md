## What this is

**IPSC Rifle** paper scoring uses **A / C / D** zones on approved **Rifle** targets (**appendices**). **Major** vs **Minor** shifts **multipliers** and how misses / no-shoots hurt — per **9.5** tables in **Rifle Jan 2026**. **C180** = handgun; **distances** and **target faces** differ — always **Rifle** PDF.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Identify** the **exact** target model and **distance** the **WSB** specifies before scoring.
2. **Apply** **9.5 Rifle** zone boundaries — not pistol-sized intuition.
3. **Log** zones consistently for **multi-position** stages (same target type across positions unless **WSB** says otherwise).
4. **Major/Minor** comes from **division** — RO **scores holes**, not **PF** class on the line.
5. **Disputed** boundaries — **overlay** + **CRO/RM** per match policy.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **9.5 Rifle** + appendices; **9.4 Rifle** for point mapping.
