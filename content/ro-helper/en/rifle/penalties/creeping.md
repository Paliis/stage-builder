## What this is

**Creeping** is prohibited **movement** or **lean** of body or **long gun** **before** the start signal that gives a readiness advantage for the first shot. **Rifle Jan 2026** addresses this under **10.2.6** (confirm exact wording, exceptions, and relation to **Make ready** / **Are you ready** in your PDF). Long-gun pre-start shifts (stock, muzzle, hips) can look different from handgun — still apply **Rifle** text.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Watch** the ready position and command sequence per match SOP and **WSB** extras.
2. **Record** movement before **Start**; note if **WSB** restricts leaning or foot lift.
3. **Apply** **10.2.6** after PDF check; escalate to **CRO** / **RM** if borderline.
4. **Brief briefly** what was seen.
5. **Document** timing relative to commands and stage ID.

## Common mistakes

- **Judging rifle creeping** using **Handgun** body cues only.
- **Calling creeping** on normal breathing or micro-balance shifts if the PDF does not treat them as breaches.
- **Confusing** creeping with **trigger prep** issues under other **10.5.x** rules.
