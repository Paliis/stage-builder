## What this is

**Significant advantage** means the competitor gains a **materially unfair** edge during the **COF**—often by shooting from a position or geometry that improves target access beyond what the **WSB** / stage design allows. **Rifle Jan 2026** routes these cases through **10.2.1.1** and **10.2.2** (read together in **Chapter 10**). **Barricades, ports, and height windows** common in rifle make fact patterns different from handgun — still anchor in **Rifle** text.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Record** actual body / rifle position vs **WSB** allowed zones.
2. **Compare** to fair execution; if unclear, **CRO** / **RM**.
3. **Apply** penalties per **10.2.1.1** / **10.2.2** after PDF verification — not generic **10.1** when advantage clauses fit.
4. **Brief briefly**; challenges per **9.6**.
5. **Document** shot linkage and WSB clause.
