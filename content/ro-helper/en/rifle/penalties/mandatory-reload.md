## What this is

A **mandatory reload** in the **WSB** means the competitor **must** perform a reload (type, point in the **COF**, count, etc.) exactly as written. If the competitor **fails** that requirement, the penalty anchor is **10.2.4** in **IPSC Rifle** — exact conditions and **procedural** count **only** from the **Jan 2026** PDF. Long-gun reload mechanics (bolt, magazine changes) do not change the rule: still read **Rifle** **Chapter 10**.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Read the WSB:** where reload is **mandatory**; single episode or several; exceptions (e.g. after malfunction) if stated.
2. **Record facts:** whether a reload occurred; whether it matches the WSB (tactical vs administrative — **per WSB and rules text only**).
3. **Apply the penalty:** scoresheet per **10.2.4** after PDF check — do not mis-bucket under **10.2.2** if the issue is reload, not position.
4. **Brief the competitor:** which WSB requirement was missed (**9.6** for challenges).
5. **Document:** WSB clause + moment in **COF**.

## Common mistakes

- **Applying** when the WSB does **not** mandate a reload at that point.
- **Vague sheet:** “no reload” without the WSB line.
- **Wrong bucket:** do not force **10.2.4** when the PDF supports a different rule.
