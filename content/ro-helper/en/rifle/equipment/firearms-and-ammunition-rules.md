## What this is

**5.1–5.8 Rifle** sets **rifle** and **ammunition** rules: **barrel** / **chassis** limits, **magazine** length, **bullet** types, **PF** procedure — all in **Rifle Jan 2026**. **C240** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Flag** obvious **banned** muzzle devices or **mag** setups per **division**.
2. **Halt** on **bore** / **bolt** damage — **RM**.
3. **Chrono** handles **PF**; **RO** does not “approve” loads by eye.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **5.1–5.8 Rifle**; link unsafe gear to **10.4** / **10.5**.
