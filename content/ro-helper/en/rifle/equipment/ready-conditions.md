## What this is

**8.1–8.3 Rifle** govern **ready** states and **RO** **commands** for **long guns** — sling / **low ready** / **shoulder** rules as **Rifle** **Ch.8** states, not pistol habits. **C230** handgun.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Follow** **Rifle** command sequence; **no** improvised shortcuts.
2. **Control** **muzzle** in **all** ready positions — **barricades** and **transitions** amplify risk.
3. **Confirm** chamber / bolt / magazine per **WSB** + **8.1** before **Start**.
4. **End** the string with **8.3** **unload** / **clear** before movement downrange.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **8.1–8.3 Rifle**; distinguish from **8.5** / **8.7** per **Rifle** book.
