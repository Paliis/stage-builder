## What this is

**Rifle** **slings** and **carry** methods are defined in **Chapter 5 Rifle** and **appendices**: allowed **sling** hardware, **muzzle** control when **slung**, and **transition** to **shoulder** / **low ready**. **C235** = handgun **holster** only.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Watch** **muzzle** when the competitor **runs** with a **slung** rifle — **10.5** territory if control is lost.
2. **Verify** **division** rules on **bipod** / **sling** combos per **PDF**.
3. **Call** **stop** on **banned** carry (e.g. **muzzle** up in crowds if **MD** forbids).

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **Ch.5 Rifle** + cited appendices; **8.1** when entering **ready** from **carry**.
