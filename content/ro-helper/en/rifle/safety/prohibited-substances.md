## What this is

**10.7** **IPSC Rifle** addresses **prohibited substances** in the context of **Rifle** competition and the range. Exact definitions, who may be tested, and **DQ** mechanics are in the **Rifle** **Jan 2026** PDF. **C135** = handgun only — do not copy sanction language across books without checking.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **RM/MD** lead on substance issues — RO supplies **safety** control and **facts**.
2. **Cease fire** if the competitor cannot safely handle a **rifle** on the course.
3. **Written, neutral** observations for **RM** review.
4. **No** public labelling — follow match **integrity** policy.
