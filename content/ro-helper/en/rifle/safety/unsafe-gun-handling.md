## What this is

**10.5.1** in **IPSC Rifle** covers **general unsafe handling** of the rifle when no **more specific** **10.5.x** or **10.4** rule applies first. Examples elsewhere in **10.5** include **muzzle angles**, **drops**, **trigger discipline** — classify there when possible. **C125** = handgun. Use **Rifle** **Jan 2026** PDF.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Stop** unsafe motion; keep **berm** awareness with long guns.
2. **Rule check:** **10.5.2**, **10.5.3**, **10.5.9–11**, **10.4** before **10.5.1**.
3. **Document** the observable unsafe act (barricade, bipod, sling) for **RM** / protest defense.
4. **DQ** only with **PDF** support.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.5.1** as **residual** in **Rifle** — read **Section 10.5** as a system.
