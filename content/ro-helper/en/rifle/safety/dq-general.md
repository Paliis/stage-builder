## What this is

**Section 10.3** of **IPSC Rifle** governs **general** aspects of **DQ**: procedure, effect, and relationship to the **specific** grounds in **Chapter 10** (and elsewhere if the PDF says so). **C130** is **handgun** only — **Rifle** competitors and **RM** paperwork follow **Rifle** **10.3**.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Pair** every DQ with a **substantive** rule (**10.4**, **10.5.x**, etc.) **and** **10.3** process steps.
2. **Safe rifle** after the call — bolt / magazine state per **MD**.
3. **Complete** the incident record: stage, time, witnesses, rule numbers.
4. **No improvisation** on who may declare DQ — **10.3** + **MD**.

## IPSC (Jan 2026)

- **10.3 Rifle** + triggering sub-rule; verify multi-gun / multi-division events in the **event** rules.
