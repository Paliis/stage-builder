## What this is

**Chapter 2** **IPSC Rifle** sets **range** safety: **Safety Area**, rifle handling off the line, ammunition, and facility rules. **C145** = handgun — **Rifle** competitors follow **Rifle** **Ch.2** (slings, cases, rifle-specific staging).

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Match layout:** know where rifles may be uncased, where ammo is allowed, and **hot** vs **cold** per **MD**.
2. **Enforce** **Ch.2** before the timer — many DQ risks happen **before** **COF**.
3. **Escalate** layout disputes (temporary bays, construction) to **RM**.
