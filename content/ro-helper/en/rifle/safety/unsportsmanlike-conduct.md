## What this is

**10.6** **IPSC Rifle** governs **unsportsmanlike** behaviour in **Rifle** competition. The **categories** and **process** are in the **Rifle** **Jan 2026** PDF. **C140** = handgun — verify **Rifle** text before citing **10.6** in protests or DQ paperwork.

## What the Range Officer (RO) does

1. **Neutral documentation:** quotes, gestures, score-table disputes — **RM** decides **10.6** applicability.
2. **Safety first:** if a conflict blocks the squad — **CRO/RM** per MD.
3. **No** mixing **10.6** with equipment protests unless the PDF links them.
